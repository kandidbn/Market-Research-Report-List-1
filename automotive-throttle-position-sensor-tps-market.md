<p><p>The Automotive Throttle Position Sensor (TPS) market is categorized into three main types: potentiometer type, socket type, and comprehensive type. The potentiometer type uses a variable resistor to measure the throttle's position through changes in resistance, providing analog signals. The socket type, often designed for easy integration, offers modularity for replacement. Comprehensive type TPS combines multiple sensing technologies, enhancing accuracy and reliability. Together, these types cater to diverse automotive applications, ensuring optimal engine performance and emissions control.</p></p>
