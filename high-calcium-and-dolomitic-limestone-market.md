<p><p>High-Calcium and Dolomitic Limestone are two types of limestone differentiated by their chemical composition. High-Calcium Limestone primarily consists of calcium carbonate (CaCO3) and is mainly used in industrial applications such as steelmaking, cement, and glass production. Dolomitic Limestone contains both calcium carbonate and magnesium carbonate (CaMg(CO3)2) and is often utilized in agriculture, construction, and as a flux in steel production.</p><p>The High-Calcium and Dolomitic Limestone Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand in various sectors including construction, environmental applications like flue gas desulfurization, and agricultural uses to improve soil quality. Additionally, the rising trend of sustainable practices and the growing awareness of the benefits of limestone in carbon capture technologies are boosting market prospects. The construction industry, particularly in emerging economies, is witnessing a surge in infrastructure development, further fueling the demand for both high-calcium and dolomitic limestone. Overall, technological advancements and a shift towards eco-friendly materials are shaping the dynamics of the market.</p></p>
