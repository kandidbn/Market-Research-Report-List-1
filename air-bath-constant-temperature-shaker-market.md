<p><p>The Air Bath Constant Temperature Shaker market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal shakers. Vertical shakers operate with sample containers placed in an upright position, allowing for efficient mixing and temperature control, making them ideal for procedures requiring high stability. In contrast, horizontal shakers position samples flat, providing a broader surface area and uniform motion, which is suitable for larger sample volumes and applications such as cell culture and solvent extraction. Both types serve specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
