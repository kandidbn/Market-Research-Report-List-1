<p><p>The single-use laparoscopic electrodes market includes various electrode types designed for specific surgical applications. L Hook and J Hook electrodes are curved for precision in cutting and coagulating tissues, enhancing maneuverability. Needle electrodes are utilized for fine tissue manipulation and effective energy delivery, while spatula electrodes feature a broad surface for coagulation and dissection. Ball electrodes provide a spherical tip for smaller surface areas, ideal for controlled dissection and tissue sealing, catering to diverse surgical procedures within minimally invasive techniques.</p></p>
