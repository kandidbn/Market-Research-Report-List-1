<p><p>The Polypropylene Geotextile market is categorized into two main types: non-woven and woven. Non-woven geotextiles are created by bonding fibers together, offering excellent filtration, drainage, and erosion control. They are commonly used in applications like landfills and road construction. Woven geotextiles, on the other hand, are made by weaving polypropylene fibers, providing high strength and stability. They are ideal for applications requiring load distribution and reinforcement, such as retaining walls and steep slopes. Both types serve critical roles in civil engineering and environmental projects.</p></p>
