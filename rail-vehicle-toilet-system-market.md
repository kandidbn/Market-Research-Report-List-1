<p><p>The rail vehicle toilet system market primarily consists of two types: vacuum toilet systems and pressured water flushing toilet systems. Vacuum toilet systems utilize negative pressure to efficiently remove waste, minimizing water usage and reducing the overall weight of the vehicle. In contrast, pressured water flushing systems rely on water pressure to flush waste, providing a more traditional approach but using more water. Both systems are designed to meet hygiene standards while addressing the varying needs of modern rail vehicles.</p></p>
