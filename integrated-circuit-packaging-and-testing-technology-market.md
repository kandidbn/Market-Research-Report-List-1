<p><p>Integrated Circuit Packaging and Testing Technology encompasses two primary market types: IDM (Integrated Device Manufacturer) Mode and Foundry Mode. In IDM Mode, companies design, manufacture, and package their own ICs, ensuring tight control over quality and performance. Conversely, Foundry Mode allows companies to focus on design while outsourcing manufacturing and packaging to specialized foundries. This division enables flexibility and cost-efficiency, with IDM providing comprehensive solutions and foundries offering scalable services to various semiconductor clients, adapting to diverse market demands.</p></p>
