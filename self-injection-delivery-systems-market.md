<p><strong>The Self-Injection Delivery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intramuscular</li><li>Intravenous</li><li>Sub-Cutaneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-injection delivery systems encompass various methods for administering medication, with the primary types being intramuscular, intravenous, and subcutaneous. Intramuscular injections deliver drugs deep into muscle tissues, providing rapid absorption and effectiveness, often used for vaccines and hormones. Intravenous systems involve direct delivery into the bloodstream, enabling immediate effect and precise control over dosing, typical in hospital settings. Subcutaneous injections introduce medication into the tissue layer between the skin and muscle, offering ease of use for chronic conditions like diabetes.</p></p>
