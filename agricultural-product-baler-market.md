<p><p>The agricultural product baler market comprises two main types: round balers and square balers. Round balers produce cylindrical bales, typically easier to handle and ideal for roughage like hay and straw. Square balers create rectangular bales, which are more compact and easier to stack and transport. Both types are widely used in farming operations to efficiently package and store forage, enhancing ease of use and preservation. The choice between them often depends on the specific agricultural needs and storage capabilities of farmers.</p></p>
